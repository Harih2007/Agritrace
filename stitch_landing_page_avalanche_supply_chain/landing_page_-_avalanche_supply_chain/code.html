The Product Journey

Follow the path of your food from the fields to your plate in five simple, transparent steps.

agriculture

1. Registration by Farmer

A new batch of produce is registered, capturing details like origin, organic certification, and harvest date.

database

2. Data Secured on Avalanche

All information is instantly recorded on the Avalanche blockchain, creating an unchangeable digital record.

qr_code

3. Unique QR Code Generated

A unique QR code is created for the batch, serving as its digital identity throughout the supply chain.

local_shipping

4. Scanned at Each Stage

From transport to processing, each handover is recorded with a simple scan, updating the product's journey log.

The final consumer scans the QR code to access the complete, verifiable history of their food, fostering ultimate trust.
